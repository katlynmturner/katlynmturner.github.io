The ECJ Community of Practice is a community of policymakers, advocates, and scientists working to advance environmental and climate justice & equity and systems change in their work. In collaboration with Environmental Defense Fund, this course offered sessions on critical theory in research and systems analysis towards the design of just and equitable climate solutions.
